Our Philosophy

Obsessed with The Craft

Ember & Oak was born from a simple conviction: Nairobi had world-class beef and world-class wood, but nowhere treating them with the respect they deserved. We changed that in 2018 when we opened in a converted colonial warehouse in Karen.

Every cut is sourced from a small number of farms in the Rift Valley and the Mount Kenya region. Every cow we serve, we can name the farm. Every steak is dry-aged in-house in our custom salt chamber. We use six native hardwoods — olive, acacia, marula, leleshwa, cedar, and muhuhu — each bringing its own character to the fire.
